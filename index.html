Short bio

I am currently a PhD student working in the speech group, KU Leuven, Belgium , under the supervision of Prof. Dirk Van Compernolle. My work focus on example based speech recognition.

I finished my master in 2009 in the State University of Petrozavodzk (PetrSU), Russia, in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science. I worked on projects about image analysis and recognition (on the example of petroglyphs), and started my PhD in October 2010.
